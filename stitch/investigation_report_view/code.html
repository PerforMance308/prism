calendar_today Oct 24, 2023

Summary

At approximately 3:15 PM UTC, the auth-service experienced a sudden 450% increase in p99 latency, peaking at 4.2s. AI analysis indicates this was triggered by a cascading retry storm following a database connection pool exhaustion in the identity-db cluster. The system automatically initiated partial throttling, but full recovery required manual intervention to clear the queue.
